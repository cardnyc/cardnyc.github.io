Christian Action for Renewal & Development (C.A.R.D) is a not-for-profit organization committed to serving Seniors and Youth in the New York Metropolitan area through Intergenerational Programs by way of Affordable Housing, Health Care and Education.

Services

The Organization at Work.

It is the goal of C.A.R.D to offer housing accommodations designed to provide freedom, privacy and security for older adults. We develop programs that support their medical, emotional, social, recreational and spiritual needs.

Seniors unable to live independently will get the support they need at individually customized levels. We work in concert with seniors and their families to ensure each person's needs are met and that services are adjusted as those needs change.




  1. C.A.R.D creates a community of positive role models and peer relationships for our young people.
  2. We offer opportunities for development and leadership.
  3. Provides peer group support for academic and social issues.
  4. Granted over $27,000 in Educational Scholarship for first year college students.
