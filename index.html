Christian Action for Renewal & Development (C.A.R.D) is a not-for-profit organization committed to serving Seniors and Youth in the New York Metropolitan area through Intergenerational Programs by way of Affordable Housing, Health Care and Education.

Services

The Organization at Work.

It is the goal of C.A.R.D to offer housing accommodations designed to provide freedom, privacy and security for older adults. We develop programs that support their medical, emotional, social, recreational and spiritual needs.

Seniors unable to live independently will get the support they need at individually customized levels. We work in concert with seniors and their families to ensure each person's needs are met and that services are adjusted as those needs change.




  1. C.A.R.D creates a community of positive role models and peer relationships for our young people.
  2. We offer opportunities for development and leadership.
  3. Provides peer group support for academic and social issues.
  4. Granted over $30,000 in Educational Scholarship for first year college students.


Our Mission

You Can Make a Difference and Impact Lives!



    CARD hosts a range of events each year, from awards ceremonies to senior luncheons that helps to fulfill our mission. Below are a few of our annual events:
    Each March, the Bishop Stanley F. Thomas Annual Lenten Concert features talented performers from our community. The first major fundraising event each year, it sets the tone for the year ahead.
    The Annual Senior Luncheon features an afternoon of dining and socializing with friends new and old. We invite students because it’s important for the young people to interact with our senior citizens in a different capacity than they may be used to. Many of them don’t get the chance to be with seniors that often, and this is a chance for them to do so in a relaxed, friendly environment. I also think it’s a nice opportunity for them to give back to their community.
    The Rev. Leslie G. McKoy Scholarship Awards Banquet recognizes outstanding student achievement. This award acknowledges the effort and accomplishments of the students through their High School career. It is also an opporunity to honor committed professionals in our community for their support of CARD through the year.
